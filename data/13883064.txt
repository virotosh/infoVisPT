Elouise is a story about a girl who moves into 221C Baker Street. With ties to Miss Hudson, Elouise meets the ever kind John Watson and his roommate Sherlock Holmes. Appearing shy and timid creates an air of disinterest to the detective and yet the blogger finds the girl charming, intriguing even. Eventually a situation arises that drags Sherlock into Elouise's path. Is she really as crazy or mad as some people claim or is she that shy girl John finds so charming? Find out in this story!
Please enjoy.
